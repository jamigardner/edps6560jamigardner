Hello!!

Welcome to my page! I'm an Instructional Technology Trainer for Utah Education Network. I'm passionate about helping teachers use the 4Cs in the classroom by implementing digital portfolios and Breakout EDU immersive learning games. I enjoy working with teachers to effectively use Canvas and Utah's Online Library and I'm the state's expert for the college and career planning website, UtahFutures.org. I have a Bachelor's degree in Marketing and I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Instructional Design and Educational Technology. Prior to joining UEN, I was a high school Business and Financial Literacy teacher.
